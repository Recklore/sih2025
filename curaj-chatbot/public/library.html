Wiley: Access has been activated for Wiley’s 908 Journals Collection to CURaj from March 14, 2023

QuillBot: Academic writing platform. The Trial Access to QuillBot (Academic writing tool) - Premium access 2023 for the Central University of Rajasthan is available until May 8th, 2023.

1) Visit the QuillBot webpage, sign up using your official email ID, and set up a password. 2) Click on the verification email you received to activate Premium.

Follow the User Guide, which consists of short and crisp tutorials on tool features, demo videos, and related blogs for learning and writing.
